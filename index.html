Core Components

Raspberry Pi 4B

The brain of our robot - a powerful single-board computer running the main control algorithms.

CPU: Quad-core ARM Cortex-A72
RAM: 8GB LPDDR4
Storage: 64GB microSD

HD Camera Module

High-definition camera with night vision capability for continuous surveillance.

Resolution: 1080p @ 30fps
Field of View: 62.2° diagonal
Night Vision: IR LED illumination

Servo Motors

Precision servo motors for camera pan/tilt and wheel steering mechanisms.

Type: SG90 Micro Servo
Torque: 2.5kg·cm
Rotation: 180° range

Ultrasonic Sensors

HC-SR04 sensors for obstacle detection and distance measurement during navigation.

Range: 2cm - 400cm
Accuracy: ±3mm
Frequency: 40kHz
